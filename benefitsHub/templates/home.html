About BenefitsHub

BenefitsHub is a flask web application where you can explore and apply for all current benefits offered by goverment agencies in Nigeria to help sustain people who are currently unemployed. Do not lose out on government intervention because you missed the information.

It is your one-stop shop for everything empowerment, grants, low-interest loans and a wide range of other benefits offered by the government. We update you with all programs of government that is aimed as a palliative to cushion the effect of the global economic downturn.

Simply create an account and login to apply and track the status of your application.

Current benefits

The following are the current benefits offered by the government in Nigeria.


Npower

The Npower program engages graduates who are attached to various sectors to gain work experience and improve productivity.


3MTT

The 3MTT program is aimed at training million tech talents, who will become skilled with in-demand tech skills in various field.
