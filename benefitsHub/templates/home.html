About BenefitsHub Nigeria

The inspiration for BenefitsHub Nigeria came from the lack of centralized information about government benefits. It is a website where you can explore and apply for all current benefits offered by goverment agencies in Nigeria. As you know, Information is crucial if you must become aware of government programs that you may benefit from, as of now, there is no single website that provides a well-curated list of available government benefits. BenefitsHub seeks to fill that vacuum by curating all government schemes and programs that are meant to assist citizens, especially unemployed citizens. It is your one-stop shop for everything empowerment, grants, low-interest loans and a wide range of other benefits offered by the government. We update you with all programs of government that are aimed as a palliative to cushion the effect of the global economic downturn. Do not lose out on any government intervention because you missed the information. Simply create an account and login to apply and track the status of your application.

Our mission is to help Nigerians access available government benefits more easily and efficiently. This project was developed as a Portfolio Project for alx Africa.
